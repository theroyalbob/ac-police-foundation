About the Foundation

The Atlantic City Police Foundation was established by current and former police officers as well as business and civic leaders as an independent, non-profit organization to improve public safety in Atlantic City by assisting the City of Atlantic City and The Atlantic City Police Department in funding for worthwhile police programs, initiatives, training and equipment that would otherwise not be feasible under the City budget.

The role of the Foundation is not to displace city or state funding for basic law enforcement, or to reduce in any way the responsibility of the City of Atlantic City or the State of New Jersey to commit the necessary funding for law enforcement services for its citizens/visitors.

Our Focus

Modern Law Enforcement

Supporting ACPD in staying current with training, technology, and equipment as law enforcement tasks become increasingly complex.

Innovation & Technology

Providing resources for ACPD to become pioneers and innovators in rapidly evolving areas of technology, strategies, and training.

Strategic Programs

Working closely with ACPD leadership to develop and fund high-priority law enforcement needs beyond the scope of the City budget.
